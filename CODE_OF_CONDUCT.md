# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making CareerBoost AI a respectful, inclusive, and
professional open-source project. Everyone participating in this project is
expected to communicate with clarity, patience, and respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Give constructive feedback focused on the work.
- Accept feedback with good faith.
- Keep discussions aligned with the project goals.
- Protect user privacy and avoid sharing sensitive data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Public or private sharing of another person’s private information.
- Dismissive or hostile review comments.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Posting secrets, private resumes, or sensitive user data.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or
limit participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented when appropriate, and
focused on preserving a professional engineering environment.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer. Include relevant
context, links, screenshots, or timestamps when available. Reports will be
reviewed with discretion and handled in a way that protects the safety and
privacy of affected people.
